Clothes Circle

Mission Statement:

An Organization under United Nations - Clothes Circle. Which will help to break a barrier
between rich and poor clothing needs. Specifically it seeks to address UN's 17 Sustainable goals.
These Include:

1) No Poverty
2) Decent Work and Economic Growth
3) Responsible Consumption and Production







How it Works: Process


Step 1: Contracting few distribution centers nearby who have high online return rate and are planning on trashing their items.

Step 2: Make an appointment with Clothes Circle using online booking system.

Step 3: Pack identical clothing items in bags and attach booking number on the top of each bag.

Step 4: A Clothing Circle truck will collect your items.

Step 5: We will sort the items based on conditions (damaged or good) Damaged items will be sent to Oxfam to be recycled good items will be re-circled to impoverished communities.
